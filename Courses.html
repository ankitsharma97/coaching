NEEV Batch
The NEEV batch designed specifically for students in grades 9 and 10 across UP (Uttar Pradesh Board), CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education), and ICSE (Indian Certificate of Secondary Education) focuses on comprehensive board exam preparation. This program integrates traditional classroom teaching with modern educational technologies, including lab experiments and smart classes, to enhance the learning experience. Here’s an in-depth look at the NEEV batch for board preparations:

Thorough Syllabus Coverage

  • Ensure complete and thorough understanding of the syllabus as prescribed by the respective boards (UP, CBSE, ICSE).
  • Focus on critical topics that are frequently tested in board exams.

Practical Understanding through lab experiment

  • Facilitate hands-on learning experiences to help students grasp scientific concepts better.
  • Conduct regular lab experiments aligned with the syllabus to solidify theoretical knowledge.

Enhanced Learning with Smart Classes

  • Use technology to provide an interactive and engaging learning environment.
  • Incorporate multimedia content such as videos, animations, and interactive simulations to explain complex concepts.
YAKEEN Batch
The YAKEEN batch is an intensive academic program designed specifically for students in the 11th and 12th grades who are preparing for their board examinations under the UP (Uttar Pradesh Board), CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education), and ICSE (Indian Certificate of Secondary Education) boards. This program focuses exclusively on board preparation, providing a structured approach to mastering the syllabus and excelling in board exams. Here’s a detailed explanation of the YAKEEN batch:

Board Exam Mastery

  • Ensure thorough understanding and retention of the entire board syllabus for each subject.
  • Equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to perform exceptionally well in their board exams.

Comprehensive Preparation

  • Provide extensive practice through Previous Years’ Questions (PYQs) and Daily Practice Papers (DPP).
  • Regular doubt clearing sessions to address any gaps in understanding and enhance learning.

Doubt Clearing Sessions

  • Scheduled sessions dedicated to resolving students’ doubts and questions.
  • Encouragement of an interactive learning environment where students feel comfortable asking questions.
